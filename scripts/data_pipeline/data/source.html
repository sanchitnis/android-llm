REVA
RESEARCH CENTRE

Specialised research hubs powering interdisciplinary collaboration and innovation.

REVA-Techno Centre

Offering cutting-edge engineering solutions, it specialises in design, analysis, manufacturing, electronics, and product development, serving defence, public sectors, industry R&D, and entrepreneurial ventures with global expertise

Centre for Advanced Materials

Focused on next-generation materials, it pioneers innovations in photonics, biophotonics, and energy harvesting, uniting science and engineering to enable sustainable technologies, advanced diagnostics, and optoelectronic applications.

Central Instrumentation Facility

Equipped with XRD, SEM, UV-Vis-NIR, photoluminescence, and Raman tools, it supports engineering, science, and multidisciplinary research, driving innovation, discovery, and impactful outcomes
